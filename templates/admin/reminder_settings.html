How It Works

🎯 Automated Scheduling

The reminder system automatically runs in the background every {{ settings.frequency_hours }} hour(s). It scans all mentor and mentee profiles to identify those with incomplete profiles and sends personalized emails.

💌 Personalized Emails

Each email is unique with 5 different styles (Friendly, Professional, Motivational, Achievement, Community). Subjects and content rotate to avoid repetition and keep users engaged.

⚡ Smart Logic

System won't email users with 100% complete profiles. Maximum 1 reminder per user per day. Tracks improvement and mentions progress when users are getting closer to 100%.

📊 Complete Logging

Every sent reminder is logged with user ID, completion %, missing fields, email content, and style used. Full audit trail available in reminder logs.

Email Styles Used

🎯 Friendly

Warm, conversational tone with emojis. Great for engagement.

📊 Professional

Formal business tone with data tables. Professional approach.

💪 Motivational

Energetic, goal-oriented tone. Pushes users forward.

🏆 Achievement

Gamified with milestones. Celebrates progress made.

🤝 Community

Community-focused with social proof. Together we grow.

🔄 Random Rotation

Each user gets a random style. Never repeats immediately.
